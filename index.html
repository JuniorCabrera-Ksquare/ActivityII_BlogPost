Santiago de los Caballeros

"The Heart City"

Santiago de los Caballeros is the capital of the province of Santiago and the second most important city in the Dominican Republic. Founded in 1495 during the first wave of Spanish colonization of the New World. Santiago de los Caballeros is the second most important city in the Dominican Republic, located in the north-central region of the country known as the Cibao Valley, about 155 km northwest of Santo Domingo and at an average altitude of 178 meters above sea level.

Food

You can appreciate the influence of Spanish gastronomy, Taino Indians and even African gastronomy in Santiago de los Caballeros, D.R. However, one of the dishes that identify the city of Santiago and one of the most exquisite is the Majarete. It is very similar to a flan, but the difference is that it does not contain eggs among its ingredients. The consistency is due to the corn flour.

Sports

Although in the city of Santiago de los Caballeros, as well as in the whole country of the DR, several sports are practiced; the favorite sport that stands out is baseball, or as the Dominicans usually call it, "Pelota".
