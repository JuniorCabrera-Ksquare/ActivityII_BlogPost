KOBE BRYANT

Kobe Bryant is considered one of the best basketball players in NBA history. His way of playing, the spectacular way he moved around the court and his contributions to the Lakers made him one of the greatest stars of the sport worldwide. His life deserves to be reviewed in 8 essential facts to know the trajectory of one of the greatest players on the court that the world had the privilege of watching:

  • Kobe Bryant was born in Philadelphia in 1978. Joe Bryant, his father, was a professional player for the Philadelphia 76ers.

  • The Bryant family moved to Italy where his father continued to play basketball. There Bryant became a big fan of soccer and his idols were Frank Rijkaard and Ronaldinho.

  • When his family returned to the United States, Kobe entered Lower Merion High School in Ardmore. There he joined the Aces basketball team. He was the leading scorer in high school history with 2,883 points.

  • Once he finished high school he entered the NBA draft and did not plan to enter college.

  • At the age of seventeen Kobe signed a 3.5 million dollar per season contract with the Charlotte Hornets.

  • In his second season in the NBA he began to be compared to Michael Jordan, in fact both played together in a game where Kobe scored 33 points and Jordan, 36.

  • In the 2002 All-Star Game he was chosen the most valuable player among all NBA players.

  • His moment of maximum splendor was achieved playing for the Lakers, team with which he won five NBA titles (1999-2000, 2000-2001, 2001-2002, 2008-2009 and 2009-2010).
